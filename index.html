What is Alpha School?

Alpha School uses the 2-Hour Learning model: AI-powered adaptive learning that lets students complete their core academics in about 2 hours per day. The rest of the day is spent on workshops, physical activity, social skills, and real-world projects.

AI-Powered Academics

Personalized, adaptive learning powered by AI meets each student exactly where they are. No more one-size-fits-all instruction.

2 Hours of Academics

Students complete their core academics in about 2 hours per day with better outcomes than traditional 6-hour instruction.

Real-World Skills

The rest of the day focuses on workshops, athletics, social skills, entrepreneurship, and hands-on projects that prepare kids for life.

Kids Love It

Over 90% of Alpha students say they love school. No more Sunday night dread or morning battles to get out the door.

A Day at Alpha School

The 2-Hour Learning model frees up time for what matters most: developing well-rounded, confident, capable young people.

8:00 - 8:15

Morning Kickoff

Students arrive and get settled. Community check-in and day overview.

8:15 - 10:15

2-Hour Learning Block

AI-powered adaptive academics. Each student works at their own pace on personalized lessons in math, reading, language, and science.

10:15 - 10:45

Snack & Recess

Free play and socialization time.

10:45 - 12:00

Workshop Block 1

Rotating workshops: public speaking, entrepreneurship, financial literacy, coding, art, music, and more.

12:00 - 12:45

Lunch

Community lunch and social time.

12:45 - 1:45

Athletics & Movement

Physical education, sports, outdoor activities, and movement-based learning.

1:45 - 3:00

Workshop Block 2

Project-based learning, team challenges, guest speakers, and real-world skill building.

3:00

Dismissal

No homework. Students leave with their academics complete and time for family, hobbies, and being kids.
